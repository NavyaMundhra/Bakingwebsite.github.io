Lemon Cupcakes

Back to Home

Ingredients and Quantities:

  • 1 1/2 cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 2 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • Zest + Juice of 2 Lemons


Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 177 (degrees celcius)
  2. Line a cupcake tin
  3. Whisk the flour, baking powder and salt together until combined, set aside
  4. Beat the butter and sugar until the mixture is smooth (use a hand/stand mixer with the paddle attachment)
  5. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients until they're well combined
  6. Add the milk, lemon zest and lemon juice
  7. Mix everything until its well combined
  8. Fill the cupcake liners with the batter (fill each liner 3/4 with batter)
  9. Bake
  10. After 10-15 mins, poke a toothpick in a cupcake. If it comes out clean then remove the cupcakes. If it comes out with batter, bake for some more time
