Bread Pudding

Back to Home

Ingredients and Quantities:

  • 6-8 White Bread Slices
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • 3 tbsp + 1/2 a cup of Sugar
  • 3 tbsp Custart Powder


Method:

  1. Firstly, grease some butter or oil in the mold or vessel in which you planning to set the pudding. Here we are using a 6 inch cake tin.
  2. Firstly, let us caramelise some sugar. For that heat a pan and add 3 tbsp sugar.
  3. Leave the sugar to caramelize over a low heat until we obtain a brownish golden color.
  4. Switch off the flame and pour the caramelized sugar into the mold. Swirl slightly to coat the wall of the bowl. Keep aside.
  5. Take 6 to 8 white bread slices.
  6. Trim off the brown sides and cut the white bread part into small pieces. Transfer it to a mixie jar and grind it coarsely. Make sure to pulse and blend and not make a paste.
  7. To this add 1/2 cup sugar and keep aside.
  8. In another bowl add 3 tbsp custard powder add 1/2 cup of milk at room temperature.
  9. Mix well without any lumps.
  10. Pour this mix into the mixie jar.
  11. Pour the remaining 11/2 cup milk to the mixie jar and grind it for 15 seconds to combine them well.
  12. Pour this mixture into the bowl of caramelised sugar.
  13. Heat 2 to 3 cups of water in a steamer. The amount of water to be added depends on the size of the steamer.
  14. Place the mold over a stand and steam for 30 to 40 minutes or until the caramel bread pudding is set over medium-low heat. The steaming time depends on the size of the mold.
  15. You can check the pudding is set or not using a spoon.
  16. Cool completely and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours.
  17. Flip the pudding over a serving plate.
