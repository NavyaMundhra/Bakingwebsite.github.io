Chocolate Chip Cookies

Back to Home

Ingredients and Quantities:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 cup of Dark/Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 and 1/4 cup of Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 2 tbsp of honey / light corn syrup
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 2 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 10 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda


Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 191 (degrees celcius)
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper, set aside
  3. Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt together until combined, set aside
  4. Whisk the melted butter, sugars, honey, egg yolk, milk and vanilla until the mixture is combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Chill the cookie dough in the refrigerator for 2 hours then preheat the oven.
  7. Roll the cookie dough into balls, and arrage 2 inches apart on baking sheets.
  8. Bake
  9. After 10-15 mins, take the cookies out if browned on top and around the sides.
